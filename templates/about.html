About Us

Welcome to our House Price Prediction Web Application. Our goal is to provide an easy-to-use platform for estimating the price of houses based on various features like area, bedrooms, bathrooms, and more.

Our model is built using advanced machine learning algorithms and trained on a comprehensive dataset of house prices. This ensures that the predictions are as accurate as possible, helping users make informed decisions.

The application is designed with simplicity in mind, allowing you to input relevant details about a house and get an estimated price instantly. We hope this tool proves to be valuable in your real estate journey.

If you have any questions or feedback, feel free to reach out to us. We're always looking to improve and provide the best experience for our users.
